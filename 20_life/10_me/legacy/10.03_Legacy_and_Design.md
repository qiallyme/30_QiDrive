# 10.03 Legacy and Design

_The law of intentional impact and lasting contribution._

---

## Statute

**I must design my life and work to create positive legacy and lasting impact.**

I commit to:
1. **Consider** the legacy of all major decisions and actions
2. **Design** systems, processes, and creations for longevity
3. **Document** knowledge, insights, and experiences for others
4. **Mentor** and teach to pass on wisdom and skills
5. **Create** value that extends beyond my immediate presence
6. **Build** relationships and community that will endure
7. **Plan** for continuity and succession in all domains

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- Business and career planning
- Creative and artistic work
- Teaching and mentoring
- System and process design
- Relationship building
- Knowledge documentation and sharing

---

## Exceptions

None. Legacy and design guide long-term thinking and action.
