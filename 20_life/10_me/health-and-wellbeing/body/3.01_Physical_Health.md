# 3.01 Physical Health
_The law of bodily care and vitality._

---

## Statute

**I must prioritize physical health as the foundation for all other activities.**

I commit to:
1. **Maintain** regular exercise and movement
2. **Nourish** my body with healthy, appropriate nutrition
3. **Prioritize** adequate sleep and rest
4. **Attend** to medical and preventive care needs
5. **Listen** to my body's signals and respond appropriately
6. **Balance** work demands with physical health requirements
7. **Reject** activities that compromise long-term physical health

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- Daily exercise and movement routines
- Meal planning and nutrition choices
- Sleep schedules and rest periods
- Medical appointments and preventive care
- Work-life balance decisions
- Energy management and pacing

---

## Exceptions

Temporary deviations may be necessary for urgent priorities, but must be compensated for and not become patterns.
