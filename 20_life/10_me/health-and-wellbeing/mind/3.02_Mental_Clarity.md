# 3.02 Mental Clarity
_The law of clear thinking and cognitive hygiene._

---

## Statute

**I must maintain mental clarity through intentional thought management and cognitive hygiene.**

I commit to:
1. **Practice** thought awareness and management
2. **Eliminate** mental clutter and unnecessary complexity
3. **Maintain** focus and avoid cognitive overload
4. **Process** information systematically and efficiently
5. **Rest** my mind through breaks, meditation, and recreation
6. **Challenge** limiting beliefs and cognitive distortions
7. **Cultivate** curiosity and open-mindedness

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- Information consumption and media habits
- Work organization and task management
- Decision-making processes
- Learning and knowledge acquisition
- Creative and problem-solving activities
- Relationship communication

---

## Exceptions

None. Mental clarity is essential for effective functioning.
