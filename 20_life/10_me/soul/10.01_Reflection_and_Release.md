# 10.01 Reflection and Release

_The law of letting go and moving forward._

---

## Statute

**I must reflect on experiences and release what no longer serves me.**

I commit to:
1. **Reflect** regularly on completed cycles and experiences
2. **Identify** lessons learned and insights gained
3. **Release** attachments to outcomes, relationships, and situations that have ended
4. **Forgive** myself and others for past mistakes
5. **Let go** of limiting beliefs and patterns
6. **Clear** physical, mental, and emotional clutter
7. **Create** closure rituals for significant endings

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- Project and goal completion
- Relationship endings and transitions
- Career and life changes
- Letting go of possessions and commitments
- Processing grief and loss
- Releasing negative patterns and habits

---

## Exceptions

None. Reflection and release are essential for healthy transitions.
