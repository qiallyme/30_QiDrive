On January 1, 2022, SRVGroup celebrated a significant milestone by achieving approximately $1.5 million in sales. This accomplishment marks a major step in the company’s growth and demonstrates its commitment to delivering exceptional services and products.
In addition to reaching this sales milestone, SRVGroup also opened a new branch in Lafayette, Indiana, which has created job opportunities for around 20 employees. This expansion not only signifies the company's robust presence in the region but also its dedication to contributing to local employment and economic growth.
The opening of the Lafayette branch is a testament to SRVGroup's strategic planning and execution, ensuring that it continues to thrive in a competitive market.
