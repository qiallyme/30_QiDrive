This event marks a significant point in my life as I navigate my transgender identity without pursuing medical interventions. Transitioning is a deeply personal journey, and for me, it has been about embracing my true self in ways that feel right and authentic.
I have focused on exploring my identity through social transition, which has included changing my name, pronouns, and how I express myself in daily life. This process has been empowering, allowing me to live more authentically and connect with a supportive community.
Key Takeaways
• Embracing identity without medical intervention can be liberating.
• Social support plays a crucial role in the transition process.
• Every journey is unique, and finding what works best for you is essential.
