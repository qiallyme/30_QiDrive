## Insights

Creating this journal system has taught me that I thrive when I have clear structure and organization. The process of building the system itself was almost as valuable as the end result - it forced me to think about what I really want from a journaling practice. The key insight is that the best system is one that I'll actually use consistently, which means it needs to be both comprehensive and simple to navigate.
