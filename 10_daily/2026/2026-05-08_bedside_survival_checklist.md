> 
> ### 1. **Breathing / Oxygen**
> - Oxygen cannula on + positioned comfortably  
> - Oxygen machine ON + tubing not kinked  
> - Backup inhaler **within reach**  
> - Nebulizer setup ready (if she uses it)  
> - Tissues / spit cup if needed

> 
> ---
> 
> ### 2. **Medications (next dose only)**
> - Current pill organizer (correct day/time slot visible)
> - Pain med (ONLY if scheduled/approved timing)
> - Any “as needed” meds you’ve already cleared

> 
> ---
> 
> ### 3. **Hydration + Light Food**
> - Water bottle (openable without effort)
> - Small snack (crackers, banana, something easy)
> 
> 👉 Dehydration = dizziness + confusion + fall risk
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 4. **Call / Help Access**
> - Phone **fully charged** + plugged in  
> - Emergency contacts visible (you, backup person)  
> - Ringer ON and volume UP  
> 
> Optional but strong:
> - Simple note:  
>   “Call Cody first. If breathing or emergency → 911.”
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 5. **Mobility / Bathroom**
> - Clear path to commode/bathroom (no clutter)  
> - Walker / cane positioned correctly  
> - Bedside commode if she’s using one  
> - Night light ON  
> 
> 👉 This is one of the highest risk areas. Falls happen here.
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 6. **Comfort + Stability**
> - Pillows positioned (back, knees, shoulder support)
> - Blanket within reach  
> - Remote / TV / something to keep her calm  
> 
> 👉 Calm = less anxiety = better breathing
