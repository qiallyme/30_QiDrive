> Buy **Large** unless hands are small. If multiple people use them, get **Large or XL** so nobody tears them trying to squeeze in.
> 
> Do not overthink this one, Cody. The rule is:
> 
> **Everyday caregiving cleanup:** 3–4 mil nitrile is fine.  
> **Poop, bodily fluids, harsh cleaners, or messier jobs:** 5–6 mil nitrile.  
> **Budget emergency:** vinyl is okay, but it rips easier and fits worse.
> 
> Also get a small trash bag roll and disinfecting wipes with them. Gloves alone help, but the full cleanup kit saves your nerves.
