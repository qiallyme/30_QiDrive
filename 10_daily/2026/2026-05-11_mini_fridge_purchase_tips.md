> That looks like a **Sunbeam mini fridge / compact refrigerator**.
> 
> Quick checks before buying/using it:
> 
> 1. **Plug it in for 20–30 minutes** and make sure it actually gets cold.
> 2. **Listen for compressor hum** — silence can mean dead unit.
> 3. **Check inside for mold, smell, rust, or cracked shelves.**
> 4. **Make sure the door seal closes tight.**
> 5. **Look for a freezer box** if you need ice packs or frozen stuff.
> 6. **Don’t transport it sideways if possible.** If it has to lay down, let it sit upright a few hours before plugging in.
> 
> For your current situation: this would actually be useful next to your mom for **yogurt, water, nausea snacks, meds that need cool storage, drinks**, etc. If it’s cheap and works, it’s a practical buy — not a luxury.
