> ### 🔑 What actually moves your score (when added as AU)
> You only get a boost if the card has:
> - **Low utilization** (ideally <10%)
> - **Long history** (older accounts = bigger impact)
> - **Perfect payment history**
> - **High limit (optional but helps utilization math)**
> 
> If your dad just adds you to random cards with short history or mediocre usage → **minimal impact**.

> ## 🧠 How this actually works (clean + real)
> When he adds you as an authorized user:
> - That account can show up on your credit report
> - It **inherits age + payment history**
> - It helps your **utilization ratio**
> 
> But:
> - Some banks **don’t report AUs consistently**
> - Some scoring models (like newer FICO versions lenders use) **discount AU accounts**
> - You **don’t need the physical card at all** → cutting it up is fine
>
