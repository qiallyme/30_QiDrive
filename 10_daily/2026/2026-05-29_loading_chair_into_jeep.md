> Cody, **do not try to muscle-lift that whole chair into the Jeep.** That thing is heavy, awkward, and it will absolutely choose violence against your back, the bumper, or your mom’s safety.
> 
> Best options, in order:
> 
> ## Best option: use ramps
> You need **two long loading ramps** or a **single wide wheelchair/scooter ramp** rated for the chair weight.
> 
> Steps:
> 
> 1. **Back Jeep up to lowest/flat area possible.**
> 2. **Open hatch fully.**
> 3. **Fold/remove rear seats if needed.**
> 4. **Remove loose stuff from chair:**
>    - pillow
>    - blanket
>    - footrest if detachable
>    - umbrella/bag
>    - anything hanging off
> 5. **Put chair in freewheel/manual mode** only if you are pushing it.
> 6. **Use ramps at a shallow angle.**
>    - Short steep ramps are dangerous.
>    - Longer ramp = safer.
> 7. **One person steers from behind/side, one person guides from front.**
> 8. **Load it facing forward if possible.**
> 9. **Tie it down inside with ratchet straps.**
>    - Strap frame, not armrests or joystick.
>    - Lock brakes / take out of freewheel.
> 
> ## If no ramps: don’t load it
> No ramps = **bad plan** unless you have 3–4 strong people and even then it is sketchy. The chair can tip, smash fingers, bend the joystick, or drop off the bumper.
> 
> ## Better emergency workaround
> Call one of these instead:
> 
> - **Non-emergency medical transport**
> - **Wheelchair-accessible taxi/van**
> - **Mobility equipment store**
> - **U-Haul motorcycle trailer or utility trailer with ramp**
> - **Harbor Freight / Lowe’s / Walmart for folding loading ramps**
> - **Fire department non-emergency line** only if this is truly a safety/access issue and you need guidance/help, not transport
> 
> ## If you must use the Jeep today
> The clean move is:
> 
> **Get ramps first → remove detachable parts → back the chair in slowly → strap it down.**
> 
> Do **not** let anyone say “we can just lift it.” That is how people get hurt and equipment gets broken.
> 
> Also: if you’re hungry/weak, **eat/drink before doing this.** Loading a power chair while shaky is not hero mode; it’s lawsuit physics with seasoning.
