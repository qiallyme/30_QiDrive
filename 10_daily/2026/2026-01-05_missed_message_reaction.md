> A short poetic monologue designed for a cinematic mini video or podcast cold open. The piece centers on choice, accountability, and presence—delivered with calm authority rather than aggression. The power comes from restraint, clarity, and inevitability.
> 
> ## Intended Use
> - Short-form video (30–60 seconds)
> - Podcast cold open or interlude
> - Audio-over-visual reel
> - Trailer-style spoken word clip
> 
> ## Visual Direction (Optional)
> - Early morning kitchen
> - Low, warm practical lighting (stove, lamp)
> - Subject in silhouette only; face never shown
> - Slow camera push or locked-off frame
> - Cigarette smoke or steam as movement
> - Ambient vinyl crackle or room tone
> 
> ## Tone & Delivery Notes
> - Calm, low voice
> - No yelling, no rush
> - Natural pauses between lines
> - Authority through stillness
> - Delivered as fact, not threat
> 
> ## Script (Locked)
> 
> “So what is it?
> 
> You about to be my partner…
> or you about to be a problem?
> 
> ’Cause if you gonna be a problem,
> then you ’bout to have a problem.
> With the problem.
> Me.
> 
> I can be your savior.
> But I can also be your Satan.
> 
> Either way, you gonna feel me.
> Whether that’s love…
> or lashes.
> 
> That’s up to you.”
> 
> ## Notes for Future Expansion
> - Can be re-recorded with different pacing for alternate cuts
> - Works equally well with masculine or feminine voice
> - Can be adapted into call-and-response format
> - Strong candidate for recurring G & I signature line
