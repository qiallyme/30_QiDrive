### User
> what should i name the estate planning app, that has things like advanced directive, last will, living will, funeral instructions, contact info, poas, hippa auths, medical auths, ect.

### Assistant
> Name it **FinalFile**.
> 
> Clean, serious, not morbid. It communicates: “everything important is in one place.”
> 
> Strong options:
> 
> | Name | Vibe | Why it works |
> |---|---|---|
> | **FinalFile** | Best overall | Estate docs, directives, POAs, contacts, funeral wishes — one complete file |
> | **LifeBinder** | Warm/practical | Feels familiar, less scary, good for families |
> | **LegacyLockbox** | Secure/serious | Good if privacy/security is central |
> | **LastInstructions** | Direct/legal-ish | Clear, but a little emotionally heavy |
> | **PeacePacket** | Gentle | Good for consumer/family use, less legal sounding |
> | **AfterPlan** | Modern/simple | “Here’s the plan after” without being grim |
> | **ReadyWhen** | Soft but smart | “Ready when needed” energy |
> | **The Care File** | Human-centered | Good if medical + end-of-life + family logistics all matter |
> | **LegacyKit** | Productized | Sounds like something people can complete step-by-step |
> | **MyDirective** | Medical/legal focused | Great if advanced directive is the anchor |
> 
> My pick based on your ecosystem: **QiLegacy** or **QiFinalFile**.
> 
> But if this is meant to be public-facing and not just inside QiSuite, I’d use:
> 
> **FinalFile**
> Tagline: **One secure place for the documents, wishes, and instructions your people need when it matters.**
> 
> Also: it’s **HIPAA**, not “HIPPA.” Tiny typo, but for this kind of app we cannot let that one slip.
