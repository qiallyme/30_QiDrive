> ## The truth about your “separate OS SSD” requirement
> 
> That’s a nice setup, but laptops rarely advertise it cleanly anymore.
> 
> What you probably want is:
> 
> | Setup | Reality |
> |---|---|
> | **512GB OS SSD + separate 1TB data SSD inside laptop** | Best, but rarer |
> | **1TB internal SSD only** | Most common and fine |
> | **1TB internal + external SSD/dock** | Fine for backups/files, not ideal as main storage |
> | **Gaming/workstation laptop with 2 M.2 slots** | Best chance for true dual internal SSDs |
> 
> For your use, I would not block the purchase over separate OS storage. Get **1TB internal**, then later add a **small external SSD** for backups / Qi files / archives. Done. Don’t overcomplicate this.
