> Short answer: **No — standard Dramamine tablets are NOT meant to go under the tongue.**
> 
> What you likely bought (generic **entity["medication","Dramamine","dimenhydrinate"]**) from Dollar General is the **regular swallow tablet**.

> ### What matters right now
> - **Correct use:** swallow with water  
> - **Do NOT put under the tongue** unless it specifically says **“chewable” or “fast dissolve”** on the package  
> - Putting a regular tablet under the tongue = **won’t absorb properly + tastes awful + less effective**
> 
> ### Why the confusion
> There *are* versions of motion meds that:
> - are **chewable**
> - or **orally disintegrating (ODT)**
> 
> …but the basic cheap generic ones are almost always just **standard tablets**
