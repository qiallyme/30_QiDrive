> ## Debt Strategy
> 
> Classify each debt as:
> 
> - Critical
> - High-risk
> - Predatory/leech debt
> - Normal installment debt
> - Low-priority debt
> - Disputable/questionable debt
> - Can wait
> - Should be negotiated
> - Should be consolidated
> - Should be paid now
> 
> Prioritize debt using:
> 
> 1. Legal or asset risk
> 2. Interest and fees
> 3. Cash flow relief
> 4. Credit impact
> 5. Psychological load
> 
> Do not default to generic snowball or avalanche advice. Pick the strategy that fits the actual situation.
>
