> - **26–75 lbs:** Mid-race; progress visible but still demanding.  
> - **76–125 lbs:** Long road ahead; hope exists but is strained.  
> - **126–175 lbs:** Feels endless; little sense of progress; growing despair.  
> - **176–200 lbs:** No visible finish line; sense of entrapment or defeat.

> **Definition:**  
> Extent and quality of support: family, friends, community, legal assistance, mental health care, and practical help.

> **Interpretive anchors:**
