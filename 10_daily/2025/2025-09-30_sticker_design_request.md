### Assistant

> Using approximate dimensions:  
> 
> - Handle total length ≈ 165 mm (6.5 in)  
> - Handle width ≈ 38 mm (1.5 in)  
> - Push area = about half the length × full width  
> 
> **Calculation:**  
> \[
> 165 \,\text{mm} \times 38 \,\text{mm} = 6{,}270 \,\text{mm}^2 \text{ (whole handle)}
> \]  
> Half of that:  
> \[
> 6{,}270 \div 2 \approx 3{,}135 \,\text{mm}^2
> \]  
> 
> ≈ **3,100 mm²** (~31 cm², or ~4.8 in²).  
>
