> Not quite — the permanent resident card (the “green card”) isn’t **Form I-851**.  
> 
> - The actual **application form** to *apply* for a green card through adjustment of status is **Form I-485 (Application to Register Permanent Residence or Adjust Status)**.  
> - The **card itself** is officially **Form I-551 (Permanent Resident Card)**. That’s the code you’ll see printed on the physical green card.  
> - **Form I-851** is something completely different: it’s the **Notice of Intent to Issue a Final Administrative Removal Order** (basically a deportation-related notice), not at all what you’re dealing with here.  
> 
> So:  
> - ✅ Apply for a green card → **I-485**  
> - ✅ The card once issued → **I-551**  
> - ❌ I-851 → *removal order*, not residence.
