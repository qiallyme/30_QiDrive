### Assistant
> Here’s how you can shape this into something clean, enticing, and scalable for tech-phobic clients while keeping your revenue model on track. Think of it as building your own “LegalShield-but-better” subscription for business organization.  
> 
> ---
> 
> ## Your Income Model
> 
> - **Target income**: $150,000 / year  
> - **Target client count**: 10 core clients  
> - **Math**: $150,000 ÷ 10 = $15,000 per client per year → $1,250/month average  
> 
> That means each client needs to be locked into a retainer or package that consistently hits about $1,250/month. Some may be higher (your $500k client aiming for $1M revenue), others lower, but the portfolio average is what matters.
> 
> ---
> 
> ## Package Structure
> 
> ### 1. **QiSuite Starter**  
> For tech-phobic couples, micro-businesses, and side hustlers.  
> - **Price**: $500–$750/month  
> - **Features**:  
>   - Digital organization starter kit (shared drive, password manager, invoicing system)  
>   - Monthly check-in + light coaching  
>   - “On call” support up to X hours (e.g., 5 hours/month)  
>   - Pre-built templates (spreadsheets, checklists, workflows)  
> 
> This is their “Netflix for organization” package. Predictable, approachable, low-tech.
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 2. **QiSuite Grower**  
> For small businesses in growth mode.  
> - **Price**: $1,000–$1,500/month  
> - **Features**:  
>   - Everything in Starter, plus…  
>   - Custom pipeline/dashboard setup (CRM, task boards, QuickBooks or Zoho)  
>   - Bi-weekly strategy calls  
>   - Revenue growth mapping (projections + KPIs)  
>   - Priority access to your network/resources  
>   - 10–15 hours/month of done-for-you support  
> 
> This is where you double their revenue. They pay for clarity and execution, not tech complexity.
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 3. **QiSuite Pro**  
> For businesses aiming for $1M+ and complex workflows.  
> - **Price**: $2,500–$3,500/month  
> - **Features**:  
>   - All of Grower, plus…  
>   - Fractional CFO support (financial modeling, budgeting, bonus structuring)  
>   - Staff onboarding/training systems  
>   - Full client portal access (ticketing, knowledge base, AI assistant)  
>   - Unlimited coaching calls  
>   - 20–30 hours/month dedicated implementation  
> 
> This turns you into their shadow ops/CFO system.
> 
> ---
> 
> ## The Retainer Pitch (“LegalShield but real”)
> 
> Instead of promising “legal protection” like LegalShield, you’re selling **peace of mind + growth on autopilot**:  
> 
> - “For less than what you’d pay a part-time assistant, you’ll have your whole business organized, tracked, and supported by someone who already knows the systems and has skin in the game.”  
> - Keep tiers simple: $500 starter, $1,250 growth, $3,000 pro.  
> - Position it like **insurance against chaos**: no lost receipts, no dropped tasks, no missing client info.  
> 
> ---
> 
> ## Marketing Approach
> 
> - **Language**: Avoid tech jargon. Lead with outcomes: “Your bills paid, your projects tracked, your stress gone.”  
> - **Visuals**: Show before/after dashboards. Messy notebook vs. clean pipeline.  
> - **Hook**: “What LegalShield is for lawsuits, QiSuite is for your business sanity.”  
> - **Entry Funnel**:  
>   1. Free audit call → show them one scary blind spot (money leak, missed invoice, client comms gap).  
>   2. Offer Starter plan on the spot (“$500/month, I’ll fix that starting tomorrow”).  
>   3. Upgrade them to Grower once they taste the ROI.  
> 
> ---
> 
> This way you’ve got:  
> - The $500k client on **Pro ($3k/month = $36k/year)**.  
> - A mix of 9 others spread across Starter and Grower (average ~$1k/month each).  
> - Total: ~$150k–$180k/year, predictable, retainer-based.
