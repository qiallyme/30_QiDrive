> # **THE SITE MAP YOU *SHOULD* BUILD (NOT THE CHAOS YOUR BRAIN SUGGESTS)**
> 
> ## **HOME (Landing Page)**
> Centered portrait of you, crisp, framed in that glassy, cosmic Qi aesthetic.  
> Around you: a circular mind-map of your “realms.”
> 
> Each node = tappable.  
> On mobile, the mind map collapses into a scrolling ring menu under your portrait.
> 
> **Nodes:**
> 1. **Health**
> 2. **Work**
> 3. **Faith / Spirit**
> 4. **Timeline / Story**
> 5. **Family / Support System**
> 6. **Identity**
> 7. **Projects**
> 8. **Updates** (global)
> 9. **Contact / Access Request**
> 10. **Boundaries + Policies** (hidden unless they need it)
> 
> Optional easter eggs:
> - “The Mirror”
> - “Shadow Work”
> - “Journals / Voice Notes”
> - “Behind the Scenes”
> 
> ---
> 
> # **CORE PAGE TEMPLATE (How each main node should look)**
> 
> Every main section uses the same template so your sanity doesn’t fall apart.
> 
> ## **1. HERO BLOCK**
> - Section title  
> - One-sentence summary  
> - **Intro video** (30–60 seconds) autoplay-muted or tap-to-play  
> - Optional: elemental color/pattern from your Qi system  
>   - Health = Water  
>   - Work = Fire  
>   - Faith = Sun  
>   - Identity = Earth  
>   - Timeline = Moon  
>   - Etc
> 
> ## **2. NAV GRID / SUBPAGES**
> Each section has 3–5 subpages displayed in a clean card grid.
> 
> Example:
> ### **Health**
> - Daily Baseline (sleep, meds, mental state)  
> - What I’m Working On (realistic plans)  
> - Medical History (locked/private tier)  
> - Mental Health Notes (locked/private tier)  
> - AI Tools I Use (public/fun)
> 
> ### **Work**
> - What I Actually Do  
> - My Tech Stack  
> - Case Studies  
> - My Active Projects  
> - My Work Logs
> 
> ### **Faith / Spirit**
> - My Beliefs  
> - The Mirror  
> - The Seven Mirrors  
> - Rituals & Resets  
> - Shadow Integration Explained
> 
> ### **Timeline**
> - Childhood  
> - Adolescence  
> - Collapse #1  
> - Collapse #2  
> - Collapse #3  
> - Becoming Q (713 arc)
> 
> ## **3. UPDATES FEED**
> Under the subpages, a chronological updates feed filtered by category.
> 
> Each update card:
> - Date  
> - 1–2 sentences  
> - Tag(s)  
> - Link to full entry  
> - Visibility toggle based on role
> 
> ## **4. ACCESS CONTROL**
> Each page has a discrete permission level:
> - **Public**
> - **Trusted Friend**
> - **Inner Circle**
> - **Family**
> - **Private** (you only)
> 
> This is why your structure matters.  
> You can literally slide sections in or out depending on who you give the URL/passcode to.
> 
> ---
> 
> # **HOW THE PAGES SHOULD *FEEL***
> Not corporate. Not blogger messy.
> 
> **Think: elegant dossier meets sacred archive.**
> 
> - Clean margins  
> - Light motion  
> - Lines connecting elements  
> - Soft glow highlights  
> - Interactive elements but nothing heavy  
> - Everything animates in a *calm* way (not ADHD pop-ups)  
> - Each section feels like entering a different room in your mind
> 
> This whole thing should feel like an interactive biography where people are guided into understanding you without overwhelming them.
> 
> ---
>
