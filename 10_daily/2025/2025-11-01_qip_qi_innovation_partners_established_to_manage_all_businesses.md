On October 1, 2024, a major milestone was reached with the establishment of QIP (Qi Innovation Partners) in Indiana. This organization has been created to efficiently manage all business operations under its umbrella.
The formation of QIP signifies a strategic move to streamline processes and enhance innovation across various sectors. By consolidating management functions, QIP aims to foster a culture of collaboration and creativity, ensuring that all business units can thrive in a competitive landscape.
