> ## 🧾 Zoho Project: “Monthly Bookkeeping (Standard Plan)”
> 
> **Description:**  
> Ongoing monthly bookkeeping service for clients under the standard $99/month plan. Includes up to 200 transactions or 3 hours of bookkeeping time per month. No recurring meeting—only a one-hour onboarding or review call when needed.
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 📅 Project Milestones
> | Milestone | Description | Frequency |
> |------------|--------------|------------|
> | Onboarding | Initial setup, chart of accounts, and client overview | Once (start of service) |
> | Monthly Bookkeeping Cycle | Standard recurring bookkeeping tasks | Monthly |
> | Quarterly Review | Quick financial check & cleanup | Quarterly |
> | Year-End Prep | Final reconciliation and tax documentation prep | Annual |
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 🧩 Task Lists & Tasks
> 
> #### 1️⃣ Onboarding
> - [ ] Create client folder in Drive / QiKB  
> - [ ] Import prior year QuickBooks or spreadsheet data  
> - [ ] Verify starting balances & bank connections  
> - [ ] Schedule 1-hour onboarding call  
> - [ ] Note service limits (200 txns / 3 hours per month)
> 
> #### 2️⃣ Monthly Bookkeeping Cycle (Recurring)
> - [ ] Download and categorize transactions  
> - [ ] Match receipts and reconcile accounts  
> - [ ] Verify income and expense classification  
> - [ ] Update profit & loss and balance sheet  
> - [ ] Flag anomalies or missing documents  
> - [ ] Post client summary note in Zoho Projects or Zoho Desk  
> - [ ] Log time (auto or manual)  
> 
> **Recurrence:**  
> - **Monthly** on the 1st–5th  
> - Duration: 3 hours (standard plan cap)
> 
> #### 3️⃣ Reporting & Review
> - [ ] Generate monthly financial report  
> - [ ] Send client summary message or email  
> - [ ] Mark project phase complete  
> - [ ] Schedule next month’s cycle  
> 
> #### 4️⃣ Year-End Prep
> - [ ] Review all reconciliations  
> - [ ] Export trial balance  
> - [ ] Prepare year-end financial package  
> - [ ] Send summary & request final confirmations  
> - [ ] Close year in QuickBooks / Books  
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 💰 Billing Setup
> - **Fixed fee:** $99/month  
> - **Credit for prior work:** Applied as “Adjustment” in Zoho Books (for owed balance)  
> - **Overages:** $35/hour beyond 3 hours or 200 transactions  
> - **Invoice Timing:** Due upon issue, covers current month starting **Nov 1, 2025**
>
