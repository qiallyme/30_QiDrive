> ---
>

> Zoho does let you **transfer account ownership/admin rights**, but it’s not as smooth as “click → move to new owner.” Options:  
> - **Change Super Admin**: You can assign another user as the *Super Admin* for that Zoho service. Once done, they control billing, users, etc.  
> - **Organization Switch**: For tools like Bigin/CRM, you can create a new org under the client’s account, then **export/import data** from yours to theirs.  
> 
> ---
> 
> ### **3. What You *Can’t* Do**
> - You can’t just “push” a paid subscription from your Zoho account to theirs like PayPal friends-and-family. Billing info stays locked to the account that paid unless you officially change Super Admin.  
> - If the subscription is annual, Zoho won’t pro-rate refunds if you cancel and rebuy under their account. (They love keeping that money.)  
> 
> ---
> 
> ### **4. Best Practices**  
> - If you think this client will keep Zoho long-term, **transfer Super Admin to them now**.  
> - If you want to keep control but let them pay, you can switch the billing email and payment method to theirs.  
> - If you want clean separation: set them up fresh under their own Zoho login, migrate data, and then step back into a “consultant user” role.  
> 
> ---
> 
> Bottom line:  
> - **Short-term =** keep it under your account, switch billing email/card.
