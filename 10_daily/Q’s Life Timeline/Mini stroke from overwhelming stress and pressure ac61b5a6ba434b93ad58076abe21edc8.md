# Mini stroke from overwhelming stress and pressure

On January 1, 2024, I experienced a mini stroke triggered by overwhelming stress and pressure. This life-changing event served as a stark reminder of the importance of mental health and the impact of stress on our physical well-being.
The days leading up to the stroke were filled with intense pressure from work and personal responsibilities. I often felt overwhelmed and neglected my self-care routines. I ignored the warning signs my body was giving me, thinking I could push through the stress.
This incident prompted a reevaluation of my priorities. I learned that it's crucial to take breaks, seek help when needed, and create a balanced lifestyle. Mental health matters, and recognizing signs of stress can prevent serious health issues.
