This event focuses on the transformative journey of spiritual awakening through the lens of neurodivergence. Embracing one's unique cognitive patterns as a design rather than a deficit allows individuals to explore their true potential and find meaning in their experiences.
Participants will engage in discussions that highlight the significance of understanding and celebrating differences in thinking and perception. The goal is to foster an environment where neurodivergent individuals can thrive, feeling empowered to express their authentic selves.
