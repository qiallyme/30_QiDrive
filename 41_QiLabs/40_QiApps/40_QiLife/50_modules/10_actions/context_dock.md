# Actions: Context Dock Integration
When viewing an Action, the **Context Dock** sidebar retrieves:
*   Associated **Knowledge Items** (e.g. runbooks or procedures for that specific task type).
*   Related **People** (contacts relevant to the action).
*   Linked **Documents** (receipts, scans, or forms required to complete the task).
