# Actions: AI Behaviors
*   **Step Generation**: Uses `generate_action_steps` to parse the description and output a checklist of Steps.
*   **Triage Suggestion**: AI recommends energy cost, context tags, and priority based on historic completions.
*   **Review Flow**: Draft steps generated by the AI are presented with dashed borders for Cody to approve before being saved to the database.
