# Why This Architecture

QiFinance must avoid the classic MVP trap: building a single-user tracker that later has to be rewritten for real users.

The architecture assumes:

- Single-user operation now
- Static or fake user/workspace IDs in early development
- Real authentication later
- Workspace-based ownership from the beginning
- Supabase/Postgres as source of truth
- Double-entry accounting model under the hood
- Human-readable UI on top

This prevents painful rewrites when the app moves from Cody-only to friend-testable alpha.
