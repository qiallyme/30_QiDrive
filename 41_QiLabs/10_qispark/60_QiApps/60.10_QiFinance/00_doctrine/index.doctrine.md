# QiFinance Doctrine

## Purpose

QiFinance exists to translate messy real-life money movement into clear financial truth.

The app is designed first for people whose personal and business finances overlap: gig workers, freelancers, caregivers, consultants, creators, and small operators who need practical financial clarity without learning accounting jargon first.

## Core Belief

Most people do not need simpler accounting. They need an accounting engine hidden behind language they actually use.

QiFinance should let a user answer:

- Where did my money go?
- What did I actually earn?
- What was business, personal, mixed, reimbursable, debt, transfer, or tax-related?
- How much runway do I have?
- What should I do next?

## Product Shape

QiFinance is built as:

1. Personal ledger
2. Gig worker finance tracker
3. Double-entry accounting engine under the hood
4. Friend-testable alpha
5. Multi-user product later

## Non-Negotiable Direction

Build ugly if needed. Do not build brittle.

Every early decision should preserve the path toward multi-user support, bank imports, receipt attachment, mileage tracking, tax reporting, and clean export.
