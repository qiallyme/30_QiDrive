# Glossary

## Account

A bucket used by the ledger to track money, income, expense, debt, assets, or equity.

## Split

A line inside a transaction showing where money came from or went.

## Debit

Accounting entry that increases assets/expenses or decreases liabilities/equity/income.

## Credit

Accounting entry that increases liabilities/equity/income or decreases assets/expenses.

## Transfer

Movement between accounts that is not income or expense.

## Runway

How long available cash can cover expected expenses.
