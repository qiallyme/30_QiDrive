# Agent Copilot Architecture
QiLife is not primarily a manual app with AI features. It is an AI Life Copilot with a Personal LifeDesk cockpit.

The highest-priority workflow is seamless capture, ingestion, AI drafting, review, approval, structured memory, retrieval, and insight generation.

## Cockpit Home Screen

The main home screen should be Cockpit / Today, including:
- Copilot Brief
- Insight Feed
- Draft Queue
- Open Loops
- Similar Patterns placeholder
- Today’s Actions
- Waiting On
- Storage/Sync health warnings
