# QiNexus

## Overview

QiNexus is the storage backbone and folder discipline layer. It provides the durable workspace for life, projects, knowledge, data, reference material, and archive records.

This structure is mapped inside your personal directory `C:\QiLabs\50_QiNexus\My Drive\`.

## Responsibilities

- Folder discipline.
- Durable storage categories.
- Bucket alignment for QiLife.
- Archive placement.
- Reference material placement.
- Data exports and structured datasets.

## Flows

```text
Item needs storage
  -> identify bucket/domain
  -> place in matching QiNexus location
  -> link from QiLife record when applicable
  -> archive only when inactive or historical
```

## Structure & Table of Contents

Canonical buckets have decimal-ordered prefixes to maintain alphabetical and logical layout:

1. [00 Inbox](00_inbox/): Raw intake waiting for triage
2. [01 Workbench](01_workbench/): Active project files and working material
3. [02 Timeline](02_timeline/): Date-ordered chronology, journals, and logs
4. [03 Life](03_life/): Personal, household, wellness, and ops domains
5. [04 People](04_people/): Relationship, family, and support network context
6. [05 Business](05_business/): Business operations and projects
7. [06 Finance](06_finance/): Money, taxes, and financial records
8. [07 Legal](07_legal/): Legal matters and formal records
9. [08 Tech](08_tech/): Technical systems, server configs, and operations
10. [09 Assets](09_assets/): Durable asset records
11. [10 Data](10_data/): Structured datasets and exports
12. [11 Reference](11_reference/): Reusable reference material
13. [12 Archive](12_archive/): Historical QiNexus records
