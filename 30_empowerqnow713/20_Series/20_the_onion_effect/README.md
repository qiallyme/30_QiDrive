# The Onion Effect

A personal essay series exploring how small, persistent frictions compound over time to create measurable effects on our lives, relationships, and sense of belonging.

## Series Overview

**The Onion Effect** examines the invisible tax of being different—the slow, grinding friction of living in a world where the defaults don't fit you, and where your preferences are framed as inconveniences.
