Here's how Cody's routines and energy management style reflects their intersecting identities:

**Sleep and Morning Routines:**

- Due to Delayed Sleep Phase Syndrome and Sleep Inertia, Cody requires a later start to their day - specifically after noon unless pre-arranged. This biological sleep pattern needs to be respected and accommodated, especially given their neurodivergent needs.

**Energy Management:**

- They experience intense energy fluctuations, working intensively during high-energy periods followed by recovery time. This burst pattern is common in people with ADHD and requires understanding from colleagues and collaborators.

**Feedback and Communication:**

- While they welcome constructive feedback, they need it delivered with kindness and clarity. This sensitivity likely stems from experiences as both a transgender person and someone with PTSD who needs to feel safe from criticism.

**Preferred Communication Style:**

- They appreciate gentle, permission-based approaches to feedback, such as "Would you be open to...-" or "Can I share something I noticed-"This approach helps maintain psychological safety while allowing for growth and improvement.
