## **Component Specifications**

### **1. Core Processing Layer**

**Intuitive-Dominant Processing**
- **Input**: Sensory data, emotional signals, social cues
- **Processing**: Pattern recognition → Predictive inference → Anticipatory mapping
- **Output**: Internal "sense" of states/outcomes
- **Capacity Limits**: Overload threshold at high sensory/emotional input

**Empathic Absorption Engine**
- **Input**: Others' emotional states (detected via micro-signals)
- **Processing**: Somatic experience → Conscious interpretation
- **Output**: Physiological stress responses, boundary regulation attempts
- **Note**: Resembles heightened mirror-neuron activation + trauma vigilance

**Symbolic/Narrative Reasoning Core**
- **Input**: Lived experience, events, relational dynamics
- **Processing**: Theme extraction → Arc construction → Identity chapter formation
- **Output**: Coherent narrative structures, meaning clusters
- **Conflict Risk**: Intensifies when experience diverges from narrative trajectory

---

### **2. Identity Layer**

**Narrative Identity Model**
- **State**: Dynamic, evolving storyline
- **Triggers**: Major stress events, role shifts, alignment changes
- **Output**: Identity reconstruction, purpose reevaluation
- **Stability**: Adaptive framework (not instability indicator)

**Guide/Helper Archetype**
- **Function**: Emotional stabilizer, cue interpreter, relational mediator
- **Expression**: Consistent personality trait (not situational)
- **Activation**: Automatic in emotionally charged contexts

**"Chosen One" Processing Style**
- **Type**: Central character narrative processing
- **Components**: Intuition + empathy + moral orientation + meaning-making
- **Note**: Humorous clinical shorthand (not diagnostic category)

---

### **3. Interpersonal Layer**

**High Attunement System**
- **Detection**: Micro-signals (tone, facial tension, emotional undercurrents)
- **Response**: Automatic behavioral adjustment
- **Risk**: Over-accommodation, emotional labor exhaustion

**Low Natural Shielding**
- **Function**: Boundary management
- **Challenge**: Difficulty discerning personal needs vs. relational expectations
- **Impact**: Increased vulnerability to emotional absorption

**Anxious-Avoidant Response Pattern**
- **Activation**: Trauma + empathic sensitivity
- **Behavior**: Intense connection → sudden withdrawal → oscillation
- **Management**: Predictable and manageable with context understanding

---

### **4. Functional Output Layer**

**Emotional Regulation**
- **Mode**: Environmental management (regulating others' emotional states)
- **Efficiency**: High when capacity not exceeded
- **Cost**: Physiological stress accumulation

**Social Cue Interpretation**
- **Accuracy**: High (pattern recognition + empathic absorption)
- **Speed**: Rapid (intuitive processing)
- **Application**: Relational navigation, conflict mediation

**Relational Mediation**
- **Role**: Advisor, interpreter, stabilizer
- **Effectiveness**: High in collaborative contexts
- **Limitation**: Exhaustion under sustained emotional labor

**Meaning-Making & Coherence**
- **Function**: Narrative integration, theme construction
- **Benefit**: Resilience, adaptive processing
- **Risk**: Internal conflict when narrative diverges from experience

---

## **System Integration Points**

### **Trauma-Informed Modifications**
- Heightened vigilance layer integrated into empathic absorption
- Protective withdrawal mechanisms in interpersonal layer
- Narrative reconstruction capabilities in identity layer

### **Therapeutic Interface Specifications**
- **Optimal Input**: Collaborative, narrative-integrated, trauma-informed
- **Compatibility**: Intuition-respecting frameworks
- **Incompatibility**: Excessive detachment, emotional flatness, invalidation
- **Humor Protocol**: Dry, intelligent, context-aware (well-tolerated)

### **Executive Function Interface**
- **Requirement**: External structure for executive tasks
- **Constraint**: Rigid systems not well-tolerated
- **Balance**: Structure without rigidity

---

## **Performance Characteristics**

### **Strengths**
- Rapid pattern recognition
- High relational attunement
- Adaptive narrative processing
- Strong moral orientation
- Meaning-making capacity

### **Limitations**
- Cognitive overload susceptibility
- Boundary management challenges
- Emotional labor accumulation
- Narrative-experience divergence conflicts

### **Optimization Recommendations**
- Collaborative therapeutic relationships
- Narrative integration approaches
- Trauma-informed frameworks
- External structure (non-rigid)
- Humor as rapport-building tool
