# Pattern Summary
A recurring trauma cycle involving an individual who oscillates between extreme victim-playing, love-bombing, explosive volatility, coercive control, and physical intimidation. This cycle has repeated across multiple episodes, often beginning with manipulation and ending with violence, fear, or police involvement.

# How He Gains Re-Entry

- Exploits Q’s empathy
- Uses helplessness (“I’m outside freezing, no one else will help me”)
- Manipulates moral frameworks (“good people help others”)
- Feigns remorse and insight
- Sneaks into the home without permission
- Leverages social pressure with roommates

# Coercive Behavior Profile

- Constant arguing, even when agreed with
- Escalation when boundaries are set
- Threats of self-harm or property damage
- Emotional hostage-taking
- Physical aggression
- Police theatrics to frame himself as the victim
- Total lack of impulse control

# Why Q Is Targeted

Q presents:
- Empathic wiring
- High openness
- Conflict-avoidance under stress
- Trauma-conditioned over-responsibility
- Desire for harmony
- Belief in human potential + second chances

This makes Q a prime target for a malignant narcissist employing victim-mirroring tactics.
