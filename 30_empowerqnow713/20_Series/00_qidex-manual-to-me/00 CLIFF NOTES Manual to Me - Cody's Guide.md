## Core Identity

- Neurodivergent, transgender person of color transitioning without medical intervention
- Lives with ADHD, anxiety, PTSD, depression, phonophobia, and sleep disorders

## Communication Style

- Needs time to process information before responding
- Prefers direct, explicit communication
- Uses silence for processing and energy preservation

## Work Style

- Thrives in async-first, visually structured environments
- Works in energy bursts - may do intensive work followed by recovery time
- Day typically starts after noon due to sleep disorders

## Support Needs

- Needs clear, calm communication with visual aids
- Benefits from gentle pacing and shame-free reassurance
- Requires understanding about forgetfulness and processing time

## During Overwhelm

- May become silent, confused, or withdraw
- Needs space to decompress
- Responds well to offers to pause and reset
