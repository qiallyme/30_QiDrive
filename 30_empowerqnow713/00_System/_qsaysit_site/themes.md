# 🔥 TRACK 2 — MONETIZATION

This is where MOST writers fail because they jump too soon or choose the wrong model.

I’ll give you the **real** ways you monetize a blog like yours — and what’s trash.

---

# 💰 1. Substack Paid Tier (the easiest)

This is the lowest-friction path.

You keep:

* free weekly posts
* paid **deep dives** or **extended versions**

Examples:

* “Chats With Consciousness — Extended”
* Shadow Work Guides
* INFJ Survival Notes
* Boundary Scripts
* Life System Templates
* Personal Growth Worksheets

Even 20 subscribers at $6–$10/mo is real monthly money.

---

# 💰 2. eBooks / Micro-books (your writing is PERFECT for this)

You already have:

* themes
* tone
* philosophy
* framing

Examples:

* **“High Voltage: A Field Guide for Overloaded Minds”**
* **“INFJ: A Survival Manual”**
* **“Chats With Consciousness — Volume 1”**

Sell via:

* Gumroad
* Payhip
* Amazon KDP

You own the rights.
Passive income.

---

# 💰 3. Coaching / Clarity Sessions

You don’t want therapy vibes, but you *do* excel in:

* pattern diagnosis
* strategy
* emotional clarity
* boundary setting
* trauma-informed reframing
* system-building for life decisions

Charge:

* $75 (intro)
* $150 (60 min clarity session)
* $300 (life architecture roadmap)

This scales gently.

---

# 💰 4. Courses or Mini-Workshops

Optional later.

Examples:

* “Rebuilding After Collapse (Your 7 Mirrors Framework)”
* “INFJ Boundaries & Shadow Work”
* “Life Architecture for Overloaded Minds”

Video + PDF + prompts.
Sell via Gumroad or Podia.

---

# 💰 5. Affiliate Links (Least emotional labor, but fits your brand)

Products like:

* self-help books
* ADHD tools
* journaling systems
* app subscriptions
* courses you approve

Don’t overuse.
Just drop links in relevant posts.

---

# 💰 6. Sponsorships (Later, after 5–10k followers)

Substack and Medium open this up once your audience grows.

---

# 🧨 The key:

**Don’t monetize too early.**
Grow the audience 4–6 weeks first.
Then introduce the paid layer.

You don’t want to look like you’re “selling pain.”
You want to look like you’re **sharing clarity** and offering deeper help.

---

# 🔥 SYSTEM FOR YOU (ADHD-SAFE)

### **Weekly flow**

* 1 main essay
* 1 mini “Chats With Consciousness”
* Cross-post everywhere
* IG reel (15–30 sec) reading the punchline
* TikTok clipped version
* Substack “reflection edition”
* Build draft queue in Obsidian
* Schedule posts (Cursor will help automate later)

### **Monthly flow**

* Package 2–3 essays as a mini-ebook or PDF
* Add a new Substack premium offering
* Record 1–2 audio notes
* Update your blog index

### **Quarterly flow**

* Publish Volume 1 of your series
* Introduce coaching slots
* Optimize SEO + sitemap
* Run a growth push
