# 📖 **Chapter One — Embrace Now**

(_Mirror One: The Illusion_)

I used to live in the future like it was my real address. _Someday_ was my favorite word. Someday I’ll be happy. Someday I’ll be healed. Someday I’ll stop repeating the same cycles. The problem is, someday never showed up. The calendar just kept flipping, and I kept waiting.

That’s the first illusion: thinking time owes you anything.

The past feels like a prison, the future feels like a promise, but the only place anything actually happens is here. Right now. Not when you’re ready. Not when it’s perfect. Not when someone finally apologizes. Now.

When you look into this first mirror, you’ll see every excuse you’ve used to postpone your own life. The “after this job.” The “once I’m not so tired.” The “when they finally love me back.” You’ll see how much of your power you’ve traded for waiting. And you’ll feel the sting of knowing the trade was never worth it.

Here’s the truth you don’t want to hear but need to:  
If you don’t take your life now, you never will.

Embracing now isn’t about reckless impulse. It’s about refusing to let time rob you of your power. It’s about showing up in this breath, this choice, this step—because it’s the only one that exists.

You don’t get tomorrow. You don’t get yesterday.  
You only get now. And if you can claim this moment, you’ve already broken the first illusion.

_Lesson: Power only exists in the present._

1. **Someday is a Lie** → Write about how “later” became your addiction and how it cost you real years.
