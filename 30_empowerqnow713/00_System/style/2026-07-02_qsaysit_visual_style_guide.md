# QSaysIt Visual Style Guide

## Core Brand Sentence

> **QSaysIt looks like divine plasma moving through ordinary darkness.**

Or sharper:

> **sacred signal, electric blues, plasma purples, low-light truth, everyday grunge**

This is not soft wellness branding.  
This is not fantasy-core.  
This is not glossy influencer spirituality.

This is:

- divine but grounded
- luminous but bruised
- spiritual but urban
- editorial but human
- elevated but still lived-in

---

## Core Aesthetic Formula

**Divine everyday grunge** + **sacred cyber-brutalist editorial** + **low-light cinematic atmosphere**

The work should feel like:

- truth breaking through pressure
- signal moving through static
- holiness inside survival
- beauty inside strain
- revelation inside ordinary struggle
- record, testimony, witness, and transformation

---

## Visual DNA

### Environment

Use environments that feel lived-in, real, and slightly worn:

- concrete walls
- dim rooms
- city edges
- late-night streets
- parking lots
- empty roads
- kitchens
- dashboards
- rooms with shadow and haze
- reflective wet pavement
- urban interiors
- archival or paper-textured surfaces

### Symbol Language

Use recurring symbolic language:

- signal waves
- electrical arcs
- plasma glow
- cracked surfaces
- gold-white bursts of truth
- mirrored surfaces
- static / glitch / signal textures
- roots / nerves / internal systems
- paper / documents / records / receipts
- light leaking through structures

### Human Presence

When people appear, they should feel:

- grounded
- resilient
- emotionally real
- not over-posed
- not glossy
- often solitary
- reflective, sovereign, or witnessing

---

## Color System

### Primary Palette

- deep electric blue
- blue-black
- indigo
- plasma purple
- violet glow
- charcoal
- graphite
- bone white

### Accent Palette

- divine soft white
- warm gold
- amber highlight
- silver-static tones

### Palette Logic

The palette should feel like this:

- darkness = pressure, memory, systems, survival
- blue = signal, thought, clarity, cold truth
- purple = mystery, depth, intuition, sacred charge
- white/gold = revelation, God, breakthrough, soul, divine purpose

---

## Lighting Rules

### Preferred Lighting

- semi-dark
- low-light
- moody cinematic contrast
- luminous edges
- soft neon bloom
- diffused divine glow
- glowing interiors within darker spaces
- reflective highlights
- shadow-rich scenes

### Avoid

- bright flat daylight
- overexposed whiteness
- candy neon overload
- washed-out pastel lighting
- sterile studio lighting

---

## Texture Rules

Preferred textures:

- worn concrete
- brushed metal
- cracked paint
- soft haze
- mist
- smoke
- paper grain
- film grain
- static noise
- glass reflections
- urban residue
- weathered surfaces
- denim / practical realism

Avoid:

- hyper-clean sterile surfaces
- luxury showroom vibes
- generic minimal tech render aesthetic
- glitter fantasy clutter

---

## Composition Rules

### Use

- strong central focal point
- editorial framing
- controlled negative space
- subject isolated against atmosphere
- bold silhouettes
- layers of depth
- symbolic object emphasis
- framing that feels intentional and declarative

### Avoid

- cluttered collage chaos
- random objects with no symbolic role
- too many competing elements
- loud text embedded in the image unless specifically requested

---

## Emotional Tone

QSaysIt visuals should feel:

- sacred
- resilient
- grounded
- bruised but glowing
- intimate
- serious
- emotionally intelligent
- declarative
- transformational
- witness-bearing

This is not “sad content.”  
This is **testimony under voltage**.

---

## What QSaysIt Is Not

Do not drift into:

- pastel therapy aesthetic
- soft self-care influencer branding
- fantasy angel-wing spiritual kitsch
- tacky occult clutter
- hyper-sexual glamour styling
- polished corporate wellness
- generic cyberpunk neon overload
- random mystical symbolism with no emotional grounding

## Master Image Prompt

```text
QSaysIt visual style: divine everyday grunge, semi-dark low-light cinematic atmosphere, deep gradient electric blues and plasma purples, subtle glowing white and gold highlights, spiritual signal energy, urban realism mixed with sacred intensity, soft neon bloom, worn textures, concrete, haze, static, reflective surfaces, emotional depth, modern editorial composition, grounded but luminous, raw yet beautiful, intimate and powerful, no glossy perfection, no pastel wellness aesthetic, no cheesy fantasy.
```

---

## Master Video Prompt

```text
Create a cinematic visual in the QSaysIt style: semi-dark and low-light, with deep gradient electric blue and plasma purple tones, subtle divine glow, spiritual signal energy moving through an everyday grunge environment. Use atmospheric haze, reflective surfaces, static textures, soft neon bloom, worn urban textures, concrete, glass, shadow, and light. The mood should feel sacred, grounded, emotional, resilient, and intimate. Modern editorial composition, slow camera movement, glowing but restrained, like divine truth appearing inside ordinary struggle.
```

---

## Short Prompt

```text
Low-light divine everyday grunge aesthetic, electric blue and plasma purple gradients, soft glowing highlights, sacred signal energy, cinematic urban realism, emotional editorial style, worn textures, atmospheric haze, modern and luminous but grounded.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
No pastel aesthetic, no glossy luxury, no candy neon cyberpunk overload, no cartoon look, no soft influencer branding, no cheesy occult props, no fantasy creatures, no overly polished beauty retouching, no bright daytime sunlight, no sterile minimalism, no random clutter, no tacky spiritual imagery.
```

## Platform Notes

### For Image AI
Use the master prompt first, then add:

- subject
- environment
- emotion
- series modifier

### For Video AI
Use:

- low-light camera direction
- slow motion or push-in
- atmosphere movement (dust, haze, glow, static, reflections)
- emotional narrative line

### For Thumbnail Design
Keep:

- one clear focal point
- stronger contrast
- less detail than hero art
- clearer silhouette or object

---

## Brand Lock Sentence

If a generated image does not feel like:

> **divine signal surviving inside ordinary darkness**

then it is off-brand.
