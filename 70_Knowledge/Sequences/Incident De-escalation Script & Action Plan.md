# Incident De-escalation Script & Action Plan

**Date:** 2026-05-24  
**Context:** Post-conflict reset to stabilize environment and redirect focus to immediate priorities (work, caregiving)

---

## 🎯 Objective
- De-escalate tension
- Prevent rumination loop
- Redirect to productive action (work)
- Maintain boundaries without re-engaging conflict

---

## 🗣️ De-escalation Script (Use Once)

> “You good, I’m good. We just got heated—it’s fine. You were heading to work—go ahead. We’ll talk later.”

**Execution:**
- Deliver calmly, neutral tone
- No added explanation
- No emotional layering
- Hand keys immediately after

---

## 🔁 If He Tries to Re-engage

**Response (repeat once only):**

> “Later. Go to work.”

**Rules:**
- Do not elaborate
- Do not answer questions
- Do not re-explain situation
- Disengage physically (turn to task)

---

## ⚙️ Immediate Action Flow

1. Enter space
2. Deliver script
3. Hand off keys
4. Begin loading task (no delay)
5. Ignore environment clutter
6. Exit as soon as task is complete

---

## 🚫 Do NOT Do

- Rehash argument
- Explain paperwork/process
- Apologize excessively
- Engage in emotional discussion
- Allow conversation to stall movement
- Add “you need to” statements

---

## 🧠 Key Principles

- **Stabilize, don’t dissect**
- **Action > Explanation**
- **Access ≠ Control**
- **Short = Effective**
- **Movement breaks loops**

---

## ⚠️ Behavioral Pattern Noted

- Over-explaining → confusion → escalation  
- His rumination → delays action  
- Your efficiency → increased pressure → conflict  

**Correction Strategy:**
- Reduce verbal load
- Increase direct instruction
- Enforce timing boundaries

---

## 🔒 Boundary Reminder

- Tasks only proceed with cooperation in the moment  
- No retroactive urgency after delays  
- No emotional processing during execution windows  

---

## 🧩 Personal Note

This was not a system failure—this was **pressure overflow**.

- High load (caregiving, finances, environment)
- Added friction (lack of cooperation)
- Result = temporary escalation

**Response was corrective (removal from situation).**

---

## ✅ Current State

- Situation stabilized
- No physical harm
- No irreversible damage
- Path forward = controlled, structured interaction

---

## 📌 Next Step (Post-Task)

- Reassess system balance
- Define contribution expectations
- Evaluate sustainability of current dynamic
