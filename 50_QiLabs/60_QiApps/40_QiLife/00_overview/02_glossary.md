# Glossary

- **QiBit**: Atomic unit of captured life data.
- **Bucket**: Hierarchical folder matching QiNexus hierarchy.
- **Thread**: Ongoing case or storyline.
- **Action**: Task or work order derived from a QiBit.
- **Step**: Subtask of an Action.
- **Context Dock**: Side panel presenting knowledge next to the active tool.
- **Knowledge Item**: Structured markdown knowledge record.
- **Timeline**: Chronological spine of all QiBits.
- **Ask QiLife**: AI layer that answers queries using the knowledge base.
- **Resolution**: Outcome or result of an Action.
- **Reflection**: Post‑action learning note.
- **Retrieval**: Search & fetch of past QiBits.
