# Calendar: Overview
The **Calendar** module provides temporal placement for scheduled obligations, events, and actions. In the **Personal LifeDesk** model, the Calendar is used to view due dates and scheduled time blocks, ensuring Cody has clear visibility over obligations without the noise of external calendars.
