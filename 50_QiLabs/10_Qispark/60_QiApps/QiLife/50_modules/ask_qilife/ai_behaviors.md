# Ask QiLife: AI Behaviors
*   **Semantic Synthesis**: Powered by the `answer_life_query` service contract.
*   **Retrieval Augmentation (RAG)**: AI retrieves records based on database links, tags, and text search before drafting answers.
*   **Interactive Guidance**: AI suggests follow-up commands (e.g. "Create reminder task to call Zai").
