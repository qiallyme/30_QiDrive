# QiFinance Product Overview

## One-Line Pitch

QiFinance helps gig workers and small operators understand where their money went, what they actually earned, and what they need to do next.

## First MVP Promise

The user can manually enter or import transactions, split them correctly, separate business and personal activity, and see a basic financial reality dashboard.

## Product Stages

### Stage 1: Internal MVP

- Manual accounts
- Manual transactions
- Split lines
- Dashboard
- Basic categories/accounts
- Export

### Stage 2: Friend-Testable Alpha

- Demo setup
- Cleaner UI
- Reset demo data
- Basic validation
- Import CSV
- Export CSV/JSON

### Stage 3: Sellable Product

- Auth
- Bank feeds
- Receipt storage
- Mileage
- Tax reports
- Subscription billing
