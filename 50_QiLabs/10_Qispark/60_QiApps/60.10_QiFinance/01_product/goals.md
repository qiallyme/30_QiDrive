# Goals

## MVP Goals

- Track accounts
- Track transactions
- Support unlimited splits
- Separate personal, business, transfer, income, debt, and tax-related activity
- Show cash runway
- Show basic income vs outflow
- Make transaction review fast

## Later Goals

- Bank import
- Receipt attachment
- Mileage tracking
- Tax estimates
- Schedule C support
- AI-assisted categorization
- Mobile-friendly workflow
