# Users

## Primary User

Gig worker or small operator with mixed personal/business finances.

## Secondary Users

- Freelancers
- Contractors
- Creators
- Caregivers with irregular income
- Small business starters
- People recovering from financial instability

## User Traits

- Capable but overwhelmed
- May not understand accounting
- Needs cash reality fast
- Uses multiple income platforms
- Has mixed expenses
- Needs tax readiness eventually
