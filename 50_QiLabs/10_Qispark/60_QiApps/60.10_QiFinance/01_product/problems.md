# Problems

## Core Problems

1. Money comes in irregularly.
2. Expenses are mixed between personal and business.
3. One transaction often has multiple meanings.
4. Users do not know what income is actually profit.
5. Vehicle expenses are central but poorly tracked.
6. Debt and overdrafts distort the meaning of deposits.
7. Existing tools are either too simple or too accountant-heavy.

## Example Pain

A user receives $7,000 and later has $500 left. The question is not “where did I blow it?” The question is “what obligations, debt, repairs, and survival costs consumed it?”
