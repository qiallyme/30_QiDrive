# Troubleshooting

## Common Issues

### Transaction will not save

Check whether split debits equal credits.

### Dashboard total looks wrong

Verify whether transfers are being counted as income or expense incorrectly.

### Friend demo data looks mixed with real data

Check workspace_id and demo reset logic.
