# Accessibility

## Requirements

- Keyboard-friendly forms
- Clear focus states
- Sufficient contrast
- Error messages near fields
- Tables usable on small screens

## ADHD-Friendly Design

- One primary action per screen
- Clear next step
- Minimal clutter
- Save progress often
