# Why QiFinance Exists

## Problem

Gig workers and small operators often live in the messy middle between personal and business finances. Existing tools either oversimplify the truth or require accounting knowledge the user does not have.

## Why Existing Tools Fall Short

Personal finance apps often fail at:

- True split transactions
- Business/personal overlap
- Gig platform income breakdown
- Vehicle expense tracking
- Cash runway
- Tax readiness
- Debt and survival context

Accounting apps often fail because they assume the user already understands bookkeeping.

## Intended User

The first user is a financially stressed but capable operator who needs truth fast:

- Gig worker
- Freelancer
- Consultant
- Creator
- Contractor
- Caregiver managing irregular income
- Small business starter with mixed accounts

## Long-Term Vision

QiFinance becomes a practical financial translator that turns messy transaction activity into usable decisions.

## Non-Goals

- Not a bank replacement
- Not a CPA replacement
- Not tax filing software at launch
- Not enterprise accounting software
- Not a budgeting toy

## Success Definition

A user can look at QiFinance and understand what happened to their money, what they can afford, and what needs attention next.
