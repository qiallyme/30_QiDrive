Superseded by ADR-0017.

## Decision

`_QiOS_DNA` is the portable master doctrine repository for the QiOS ecosystem.

It mirrors the locked QiLabs root logic:

```text
00_QiEOS
10_QiOS_Start
20_QiSystem
30_QiServer
40_QiCapture
50_QiNexus
60_QiApps
70_QiConnect
packages
scripts
toolbox
```

## Consequences

- System-wide doctrine belongs here.
- App-specific docs may live in child repos, but canonical system-level interpretation lives here.
- Raw imported repo docs stay under `00_QiEOS/receipts/raw_imports/`.
- Reconciliation notes live under `00_QiEOS/reconciliation/`.
- Generated exports live under `00_QiEOS/exports/`.
