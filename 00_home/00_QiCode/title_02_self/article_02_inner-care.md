## Article 2. Inner Care

Stable ID: `T02.A02`

### Sec. 2.02.010. Regulation

Stable ID: `T02.A02.S010`

- `Line 1` Regulation is not repression; it is the practice of returning enough agency to choose the next honest step. (`Sec. 2.02.010.L001`; stable ID `T02.A02.S010.L001`)
- `Line 2` Apply this provision with truth, care, consent, and proportion. (`Sec. 2.02.010.L002`; stable ID `T02.A02.S010.L002`)
- `Line 3` If the provision is used in a project, link back to the record, output, or decision that applies it. (`Sec. 2.02.010.L003`; stable ID `T02.A02.S010.L003`)

### Sec. 2.02.020. Rest

Stable ID: `T02.A02.S020`

- `Line 1` Rest is a lawful maintenance act within QiCode and does not require moral defense. (`Sec. 2.02.020.L001`; stable ID `T02.A02.S020.L001`)
