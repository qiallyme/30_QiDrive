# QiCode Life Protocol – Realm 1

These 21 points are **my code**.
They’re arranged in a 7×3 matrix whose sums keep collapsing back into **3**, the number of Source, Trinity, and Creation.

- 7 rows = 7 stages / lenses.
- 3 columns = 3 aspects of each stage.
- The row sums form a repeating **3 → 6 → 9 → 3 → 6 → 9 → 3** pattern.
- The total sum is 147 → 3.

When this pattern shows up in my life, I treat it as a **Qi-π moment**:
> the circle has closed; I have witnessed the lesson.
