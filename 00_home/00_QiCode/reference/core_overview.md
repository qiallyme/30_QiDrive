# QiCode Core Overview

QiCode is the personal life doctrine. It is written in a legal-code style so it can be cited, extended, and applied without losing its center.

## Doctrine Layers

1. `Title 1` establishes My Truth and Source: Law of One, EmpowerQNow, voice, and source alignment.
2. `Title 2` through `Title 4` govern the inner world: self, identity, mind, will, boundaries, and agency.
3. `Title 5` through `Title 7` govern the outer world: work, creation, people, exchange, ethics, responsibility, and disclosure.
4. `Title 8` governs cycles, closure, archive, and legacy.
5. `Title 9` bridges into QiSpark technical doctrine.

## Technical Handoff

QiSpark is the technical/dev doctrine that implements and supports QiCode. QiSpark feeds QiServer, QiMemory, QiConnect, QiVault, and QiApps.
