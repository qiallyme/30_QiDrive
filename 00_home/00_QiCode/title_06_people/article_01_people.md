## Article 1. People

Stable ID: `T06.A01`

### Sec. 6.01.010. Whole Person

Stable ID: `T06.A01.S010`

- `Line 1` A person is never only a role, conflict, debt, diagnosis, or request. (`Sec. 6.01.010.L001`; stable ID `T06.A01.S010.L001`)
- `Line 2` Apply this provision with truth, care, consent, and proportion. (`Sec. 6.01.010.L002`; stable ID `T06.A01.S010.L002`)
- `Line 3` If the provision is used in a project, link back to the record, output, or decision that applies it. (`Sec. 6.01.010.L003`; stable ID `T06.A01.S010.L003`)

### Sec. 6.01.020. Role Clarity

Stable ID: `T06.A01.S020`

- `Line 3` If the provision is used in a project, link back to the record, output, or decision that applies it. (`Sec. 6.01.020.L003`; stable ID `T06.A01.S020.L003`)
