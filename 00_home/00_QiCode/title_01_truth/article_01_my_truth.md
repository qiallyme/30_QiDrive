## Article 1. My Truth

Stable ID: `T01.A01`

### Sec. 1.01.010. Truth Before Performance

Stable ID: `T01.A01.S010`

- `Line 1` My truth is not a performance, brand posture, or argument strategy; it is the record of what I know, live, observe, and choose to stand inside. (`Sec. 1.01.010.L001`; stable ID `T01.A01.S010.L001`)
- `Line 2` Apply this provision with truth, care, consent, and proportion. (`Sec. 1.01.010.L002`; stable ID `T01.A01.S010.L002`)
- `Line 3` If the provision is used in a project, link back to the record, output, or decision that applies it. (`Sec. 1.01.010.L003`; stable ID `T01.A01.S010.L003`)

### Sec. 1.01.020. Witness

Stable ID: `T01.A01.S020`

- `Line 1` I may witness my life directly before translating it for another person's comfort, system, or category. (`Sec. 1.01.020.L001`; stable ID `T01.A01.S020.L001`)
- `Line 2` Apply this provision with truth, care, consent, and proportion. (`Sec. 1.01.020.L002`; stable ID `T01.A01.S020.L002`)
- `Line 3` If the provision is used in a project, link back to the record, output, or decision that applies it. (`Sec. 1.01.020.L003`; stable ID `T01.A01.S020.L003`)

### Sec. 1.01.030. Non-Erasure

Stable ID: `T01.A01.S030`

- `Line 1` No institution, relationship, crisis, or diagnosis may erase the continuity of my lived truth. (`Sec. 1.01.030.L001`; stable ID `T01.A01.S030.L001`)
- `Line 2` Apply this provision with truth, care, consent, and proportion. (`Sec. 1.01.030.L002`; stable ID `T01.A01.S030.L002`)
- `Line 3` If the provision is used in a project, link back to the record, output, or decision that applies it. (`Sec. 1.01.030.L003`; stable ID `T01.A01.S030.L003`)
