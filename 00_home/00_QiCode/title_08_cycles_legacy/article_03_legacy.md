## Article 3. Legacy

Stable ID: `T08.A03`

### Sec. 8.03.010. Inheritance

Stable ID: `T08.A03.S010`

- `Line 1` Legacy is what can be carried forward without needing the whole crisis to be relived. (`Sec. 8.03.010.L001`; stable ID `T08.A03.S010.L001`)
- `Line 2` Apply this provision with truth, care, consent, and proportion. (`Sec. 8.03.010.L002`; stable ID `T08.A03.S010.L002`)
- `Line 3` If the provision is used in a project, link back to the record, output, or decision that applies it. (`Sec. 8.03.010.L003`; stable ID `T08.A03.S010.L003`)

### Sec. 8.03.020. Design

Stable ID: `T08.A03.S020`
