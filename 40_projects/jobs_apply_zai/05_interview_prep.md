# Zaitullah — Interview preparation

Practice slowly with a helper. Short, truthful answers are better than memorized long answers.

## 30-second introduction

My name is Zaitullah Khebarkhil. I live in Indianapolis. I have experience in gate security, escorting personnel, observation, and working safely under pressure. I also have professional driving experience with Lyft. I am dependable, alert, and ready to work **[AVAILABLE SHIFT]**.

## Security interview answers

### Why do you want this job?

I have security experience. I like work where safety, attention, and responsibility are important. I want stable work and I am ready to learn the company’s procedures.

### What would you do if you saw something suspicious?

I would stay calm, keep a safe distance, follow the post instructions, and report it to my supervisor or the correct emergency contact. I would write down the facts I observed.

### How do you handle conflict?

I stay calm and respectful. I do not argue. I follow the rules, keep people safe, and call my supervisor or police when the procedure requires it.

### Can you write incident reports in English?

Use the truthful answer. If English help is needed: `My spoken and written English is improving. I can learn the required report format, use approved tools, and ask my supervisor when I need clarification.`

## Warehouse interview answers

### Can you do physical work safely?

Only if true: `Yes. I can stand, walk, bend, and lift [CONFIRMED WEIGHT]. I follow lifting and safety instructions.`

### How do you avoid mistakes?

I listen carefully, repeat instructions when needed, check labels and numbers, scan correctly, and ask when I do not understand.

## Questions to ask

- What training is provided before the first shift?
- What is the exact schedule and guaranteed number of hours?
- Is the location reachable by my transportation at the shift start and end?
- What English reading and report-writing tasks are required?
- When do benefits begin?
- What documents should I bring to orientation?

## Interview checklist

- [ ] Confirm address, time, parking, and contact person.
- [ ] Bring ID and work documents only when requested through a legitimate process.
- [ ] Wear clean, simple business-casual clothes.
- [ ] Bring two résumé copies.
- [ ] Arrive 15 minutes early.
- [ ] Ask for clarification instead of guessing.
