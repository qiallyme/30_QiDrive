# Role and Resignation Context
Date prepared: 2026-06-30

## Professional / relationship description
Former in-home caregiver / primary family support.

## Short form field text
Former in-home caregiver / primary family support. I provided daily care in the home. I resigned due to worsening medical/behavioral decline, escalating threats/paranoia, and unsafe conditions that required outside intervention.

## Longer neutral description
I was Lisa’s in-home caregiver and primary support person. I lived in the home and provided daily care/support beyond the limited paid caregiver hours. Due to her worsening medical and behavioral decline, escalating paranoia/threats, and lack of adequate outside care support, I resigned for my own safety because the situation had become unsafe and unmanageable without intervention.

## Key points to preserve
- I was not a random outsider.
- I had direct care knowledge and household knowledge.
- I left / resigned due to safety and escalating instability.
- I did not abandon concern for Lisa’s safety; I stepped back because the situation became unsafe.
- I do not feel safe returning alone due to threats and unsafe people around the home.
