30. Plain-English Summary for Lisa

Lisa, this package is meant to make sure the right person can help you and that your wishes are clear.

  1. Cody James Rice-Velasquez gets everything.
  2. Cody handles your estate after death.
  3. Cody handles your money and legal affairs if you cannot.
  4. Cody makes medical decisions if you cannot speak.
  5. You want CPR and all possible reasonable care, but you do not want to be left permanently as a vegetable.
  6. You do not want intubation without sedation unless there is no other choice.
  7. You do not want organ or tissue donation.
  8. You want cremation and something simple, not sad.
  9. You have a plot at Park Lawn Cemetery, but the exact plot details still need to be verified.

The Will handles probate property. It does not automatically control retirement accounts, life insurance, payable-on-death bank accounts, transfer-on-death property, or jointly owned assets. Those must be checked separately, even though none are currently expected except known accounts/apps.

The power of attorney helps while you are alive. The Will helps after death. The health care directive helps if you cannot speak for yourself. Each document has a different job.

The key move now is attorney review if possible, then correct signing with witnesses and notary. Do not let this sit half-finished. Half-finished estate planning creates the exact mess this package is trying to prevent.
