# Private Decision Log — Report Not Sent
Date prepared: 2026-06-30

## Purpose
Private record explaining why the APS / social services report was not sent at the moment it was drafted.

## Note
I prepared to make a report because I was concerned about Lisa’s safety, medical/behavioral status, possible unsafe influence around her, threats, animal welfare, and property/access issues.

I did not send the report at that time because I felt spiritually that it was not the correct moment. I chose not to escalate immediately and instead preserved the evidence and organized the case file.

## Important distinction
This note is for my private case file only.

If a formal report is later made, the report should focus on observable facts:
- medical vulnerability
- reported medication-related mental status concerns
- escalating and contradictory messages
- threats involving police/charges
- unsafe people with access to the home
- property/access disputes
- animal welfare concern involving Mr. Link
- concern that I cannot safely go to the residence alone

Do not include spiritual timing as the reason for delay in a formal report unless specifically asked and unless it is relevant.
