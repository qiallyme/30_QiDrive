## 📁 `00_Admin`

**Purpose:** Governance, authority, meta.  
**Question it answers:** _Who owns this plan and how is it controlled?_

**Put here:**

- Care Plan **README / Manifest**
    
- Authority statement (“This plan is governed by…”)
    
- Scope & intent of the care plan
    
- Classification rules (what is shared vs restricted)
    
- Any legal/administrative framing
    

**Do NOT put here:**

- Daily care steps
    
- Medical info
    
- Personal notes
    

---

## 📁 `10_Overview`

**Purpose:** Orientation.  
**Question:** _What does someone need to understand in 2–5 minutes?_

**Put here:**

- High-level care summary
    
- Primary conditions (non-detailed)
    
- Mobility / breathing awareness
    
- “What matters most” bullet list
    
- One-page intro for helpers
    

**Rule:**  
If someone is overwhelmed, this is where they start.

---

## 📁 `20_Daily_Care`

**Purpose:** Normal operations.  
**Question:** _What happens on a regular day?_

**Put here:**

- Daily routines (morning, evening)
    
- Treatment schedules (e.g., Raven every 4 hours)
    
- Hygiene expectations
    
- Meals / rest rhythms
    
- “If nothing is wrong, do this”
    

**Rules:**

- Step-by-step
    
- No explanations
    
- No emotions
    
- No exceptions
    

---

## 📁 `30_Escalation_Emergency_Playbooks`

**Purpose:** Action under stress.  
**Question:** _When something changes, what do I do immediately?_

**Put here:**

- Breathing distress playbooks
    
- Anxiety escalation handling
    
- Oxygen escalation rules
    
- When to call you vs 911
    
- What to grab (bags, keys, meds)
    
- EMT handoff script
    

**Rule:**  
If this folder is open, **speed > completeness**.

---

## 📁 `40_Tools_Equipment_Accessibility`

**Purpose:** Physical reality.  
**Question:** _What tools exist and how must they be left?_

**Put here:**

- CPAP handling rules
    
- Oxygen readiness notes
    
- Shower head placement rule
    
- Mobility aids
    
- Accessibility constraints
    
- “After use, leave it like this”
    

**Rule:**  
This folder prevents accidents and frustration.

---

## 📁 `50_Caregiver_SOPs`

**Purpose:** Operational wisdom.  
**Question:** _How does care adapt when conditions change?_

**Put here:**

- Late-night mode
    
- Fatigue simplifications
    
- Reduced-capacity routines
    
- Backup workflows
    
- “If caregiver is stressed/tired, do this instead”
    

**Rule:**  
This is **how**, not **what happened**.

---

## 🔐 `06_Restricted_Private_Notes`

**Purpose:** Protection.  
**Question:** _What must NOT be shared?_

**Put here:**

- Sensitive personal information
    
- Family dynamics
    
- Trust boundaries
    
- Notes about specific people
    
- Legal / financial concerns
    
- Your internal reasoning
    

**Hard rules:**

- Never exported
    
- Never shared
    
- Never summarized
    
- Never quoted
    

This folder exists so the rest of the system can stay clean.

---

## 📁 `70_Change_Log`

**Purpose:** Accountability.  
**Question:** _What changed and who decided?_

**Put here:**

- Date + summary of changes
    
- “Rule X updated”
    
- “Schedule adjusted”
    
- No narrative, no justification
    

**Rule:**  
One line per change. That’s it.

---

## 🔒 One Last Rule (Important)

If you’re ever unsure where something goes, ask:

> **Is this about WHAT we do, HOW we do it, WHEN we change, or WHAT must be protected?**

- WHAT → `20_Daily_Care`
    
- HOW → `50_Caregiver_SOPs`
    
- WHEN THINGS GO WRONG → `30_Escalation_Emergency_Playbooks`
    
- TOOLS → `40_Tools_Equipment_Accessibility`
    
- PROTECTION → `06_Restricted_Private_Notes`
