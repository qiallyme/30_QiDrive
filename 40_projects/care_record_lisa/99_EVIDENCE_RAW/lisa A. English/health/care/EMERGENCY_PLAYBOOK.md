# Emergency Playbook

Breathing Distress:
- Stop activity
- Prepare oxygen
- Use CPAP if directed

Call 911 if:
- Severe breathing difficulty
- Loss of responsiveness

Grab:
- Red hospital bag
- Door key from power chair pouch
