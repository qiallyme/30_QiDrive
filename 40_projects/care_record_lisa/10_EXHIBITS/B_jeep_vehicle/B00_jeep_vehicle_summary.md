# Exhibit B00 - Jeep Vehicle Summary

## Claim Supported

The Jeep should be understood as a vehicle Cody paid for and relied on, with title/use context shaped by housing and practical support decisions, not as a simple allegation of taking someone else's property.

## Current Evidence

- [[03_ledger_vehicle_value_and_use]]
- [[00_timeline_master]]
- [[03_timeline_indianapolis_period]]
- [[02_timeline_housing_transition]]
- June 27 caregiver-collapse addendum PDF.

## Key Facts To Preserve

- The addendum states Cody reports paying approximately $20,000 for the Jeep and paying it off early.
- The broader record states the Jeep was temporarily titled in Lisa's name for practical/housing-related reasons and that Lisa had use of it at no cost for an extended period.
- The general timeline records Jeep title transfer into Cody's name on June 20, 2026.

## Evidence Needed

Purchase records, payoff records, title history, BMV transfer records, insurance records, and messages acknowledging the Jeep as Cody's vehicle.
