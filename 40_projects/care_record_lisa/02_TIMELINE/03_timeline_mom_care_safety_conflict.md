# Timeline — Lisa Care, Safety, Access, and Conflict

- Lisa had significant medical needs and required support with medications, mobility, oxygen/breathing needs, and daily care.
- Outside care support was limited or did not respond adequately to the worsening situation, according to Cody.

## Decline and resignation context
- Cody observed worsening medical/behavioral decline, paranoia, escalating threats, and unsafe household conditions.
- Cody resigned from the caregiving arrangement for personal safety and because the situation became unsafe/unmanageable without outside intervention.
- Cody hoped stepping back would de-escalate the situation.
