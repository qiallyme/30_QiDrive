# Case Snapshot — Vulnerable Adult Safety Concern
Date prepared: 2026-06-30

## Person of concern
Lisa — mother of Cody.

## Reporter / source context
Cody was Lisa’s in-home caregiver / primary family support and lived in the home while providing daily support beyond limited paid caregiver hours.

## Core concern
Lisa appears medically vulnerable and has recently shown escalating, contradictory, and threatening behavior. There are concerns about possible medication-related mental status changes, unsafe people around her, coercion/exploitation risk, animal welfare, and disputed property/access issues.

## Immediate pattern observed
Recent messages from Lisa include:
- threats to involve police and press charges over her cat
- accusation that Cody took the cat after first asking if the cat was okay
- rapid topic switching between cat, Jeep, money, wheelchair/meds, social workers, Otis/Frank, and police
- statements suggesting access to the house is chaotic or contested
- statements referencing Otis being allowed at or on the lease for the residence
- blaming and threatening language toward Cody

## Risk themes
1. **Medical vulnerability:** Lisa has significant care needs, including mobility equipment, breathing medication, and support needs.
2. **Possible altered mental status:** Cody reports that a doctor previously indicated medication may be contributing to psychosis or altered mental status.
3. **Unsafe person access:** Otis is described as having a history of violence, threats, substance use concerns, housing pressure, and repeated money requests.
4. **Possible exploitation/coercion:** Otis reportedly asked Lisa for money repeatedly and may have needed housing around the same time Lisa’s tone toward Cody changed sharply.
5. **Animal welfare:** Mr. Link is reportedly missing. Cody states he saw and fed Mr. Link before being made to leave and has not seen him since.
6. **Safety of reporter:** Cody does not feel safe going to the residence alone due to threats and unsafe people connected to the home.

## Current status
No formal report was sent at the time of preparation. Evidence was preserved for possible later reporting.
