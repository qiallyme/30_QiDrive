# Record Scope and Boundaries

## Purpose

This record exists to preserve facts, timelines, communications, financial support, caregiving labor, housing/property context, and system failures related to Lisa's care situation.

It is meant to help distinguish documented context from mischaracterization. It is not a general diary, venting space, or public packet.

## In Scope

- Formal statements, cover emails, and clarification records.
- Timeline entries tied to events, care transitions, communication, housing, transportation, or safety.
- Exhibit summaries and supporting facts.
- Financial ledgers, worklogs, and caregiving labor records.
- Communication summaries and evidence indexes.
- Raw evidence preserved in archive folders.

## Internal Only Unless Reviewed

- Family conflict interpretation.
- Raw screenshots, texts, emails, and receipts.
- Notes involving medical, financial, or legal sensitivity.
- Documents that include speculation, emotional processing, or unresolved allegations.

## Disclosure Rule

Send summaries and formal records only after reviewing them for audience, sensitivity, and purpose. Do not send the full vault.
