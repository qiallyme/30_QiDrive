# Cody — Application kit

## Master professional summary

Business systems and people-operations leader with 15+ years spanning HR, IT, compliance, and cross-functional operations. Designs and improves SaaS workflows, HR lifecycle processes, integrations, data models, and AI-enabled automations. Experienced translating stakeholder needs into practical requirements, scalable systems, documentation, testing, and change plans that improve reliability, visibility, and decision-making.

## Short application summary

I build the connective tissue between people, process, and technology. My background combines HR operations, business analysis, compliance, APIs, automation, and hands-on system design across growing organizations and consulting environments.

Sincerely,

## High-value examples to prepare

Use STAR format and add real numbers where supportable:

1. A broken onboarding/offboarding process redesigned end to end.
2. An API/webhook/automation integration across two or more systems.
3. A stakeholder disagreement translated into clear requirements and a workable launch.
4. A compliance or access-control risk identified and corrected.
5. A portal, cockpit, or dashboard that improved operational visibility.
6. The MSIL workers’ compensation redesign and the evidence behind the reported savings.
