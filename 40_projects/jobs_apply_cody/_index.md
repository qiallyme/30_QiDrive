# Cody Job Search Command Center

Goal: secure a remote Business Systems / People Systems role at $85,000+ as quickly as possible.

## Start here

1. Open [[01_candidate_profile]] and confirm the facts marked **VERIFY**.
2. Apply in the order shown in [[02_open_jobs]].
3. Copy the correct material from [[03_application_kit]].
4. Update [[04_application_tracker]] immediately after every application or contact.
5. Use [[05_interview_prep]] before recruiter screens and interviews.

## Filing system

- `10_resume_and_identity/` — master résumé, tailored résumé copies, portfolio material, and a checklist of personal details. Do not duplicate sensitive identity documents unless necessary.
- `20_submissions/` — one folder per submitted application containing the posting, answers, submitted documents, and confirmation.
- `30_interviews/` — recruiter-screen notes, interview preparation, thank-you messages, and decisions.
- `40_assessments/` — take-home assignments and instructions.
- `50_offers_and_onboarding/` — offers, benefit summaries, negotiation notes, and onboarding instructions.
- `90_closed_and_archive/` — withdrawn, rejected, expired, or otherwise closed opportunities.

Use this name for each application folder: `YYYY-MM-DD_company_role`.
