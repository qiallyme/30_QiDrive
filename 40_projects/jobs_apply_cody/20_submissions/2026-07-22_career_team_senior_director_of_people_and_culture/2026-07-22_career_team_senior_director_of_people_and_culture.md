## Job Posting Details

### Role Purpose & Overview
Career Team is a leading private workforce development organization (founded in 1996) providing smart software, world-class trainers, case managers, and job developers. The Senior Director of People & Culture partners with business leaders to shape the People & Culture strategy, talent lifecycle, compensation, org design, succession, performance management, and culture across a fast-growing, multi-state distributed team.

### Primary Accountabilities ("What You'll Own")
- **People & Culture Strategy:** Shape strategy, programs, and scalable HR systems for the next growth chapter.
- **Talent Lifecycle & Acquisition:** Sharpen talent attraction, hiring bar, onboarding, and lifecycle practices across all functions.
- **Executive Advisory:** Partner with senior leaders on org design, compensation structures, succession planning, and high-stakes people decisions.
- **Scalable HR Frameworks:** Implement right-sized performance management, career pathing, leadership development, and engagement programs.
- **Multi-State HR Operations:** Direct multi-state HR compliance, benefits administration, HRIS management, employee relations, and audit readiness.
