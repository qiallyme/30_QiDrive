# Career Proof Inventory

## Strong proof

- 15+ years across HR, staffing, operations, management, and workforce systems
- 2012–2023 MS-IL tenure
- Progression from recruiter to senior account manager / HRIS trainer to Operations HR Manager
- Workforces of up to 500 employees
- $1M+ cumulative workers’ compensation and safety savings / cost avoidance
- HRIS experience with WorkForce 2.0, TempWorks, ADP, Zoho One
- Bilingual English / Spanish
- Branch audits, SOPs, policies, training, compliance, records, and account support
- Information security education and technical systems capability

## Claims requiring reconciliation

- LinkedIn: 1,500+ headcount
- Resume: up to 500 employees
- LinkedIn: 15+ years
- Resume: 14+ years

## Rule

Use the narrower, clearly supportable number unless documentation supports a broader metric.
