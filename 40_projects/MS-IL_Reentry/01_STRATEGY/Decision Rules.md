# Decision Rules

## Continue external search until

MS-IL provides one of:

- Scheduled interview
- Written next step
- Specific opening
- Defined short-term assignment
- Offer

Warm words are not a plan.

## Accept a lower-level role when

- Weekly income is reliable
- Schedule is workable
- Primary job is defined
- Reporting line is clear
- Scope is sustainable
- There is no hidden frontline supervisor duty

## Decline or renegotiate when

- Role is vague
- On-call expectations are routine
- Several leaders can assign work independently
- Pay does not match actual responsibility
- “Temporary help” has no endpoint or structure

## Communication rule

Be candid, not confessional.

Use:

> I had a difficult personal stretch and am rebuilding stability. I am ready to work and intentionally seeking a sustainable scope.

Do not provide the full crisis history unless specifically necessary.
