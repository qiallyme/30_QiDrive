# Stakeholder Map

| Person | Role | Relationship | What they can do | Ask |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Elvis | Director of Operations | Known leadership contact | Identify operational need, route placement, sponsor conversation | Consider Cody for current or upcoming focused IC role |
| Matt | COO | Known executive contact | Authorize, support, or redirect placement | Maintain executive visibility and remove unnecessary barriers |
| Tugso | Senior operational contact | Respectful love-hate history; high operational influence | Provide field intelligence and advocate internally | Explain current landscape and put Cody’s name in the room |
| Current HR lead | Unknown | No current relationship | Run formal process, screening, offer administration | Engage only after leadership routes the campaign |
| CEO | Known socially/professionally but not direct-call relationship | High-level familiarity | Final authority if needed | Do not contact in first wave |

## Contact sequence

1. Elvis — formal email
2. Matt — copied
3. Tugso — separate text 1–3 hours later
4. HR — only after routing
5. CEO — only if invited or all appropriate channels fail and a direct relationship supports it
