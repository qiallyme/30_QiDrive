# Mission Brief

## Objective

Return to MS-IL in a stable, focused individual-contributor role.

## Why MS-IL

- Known environment
- Existing trust and history
- Proven internal credibility
- Strong institutional knowledge
- Better risk-adjusted choice than starting at a competitor for similar pay
- Natural room for responsibility to grow after stability is restored

## Positioning

Cody is not asking to recover a former title.

Cody is offering:

- HR and workforce operations depth
- Staffing and recruiting experience
- HRIS and systems capability
- Compliance, workers’ compensation, safety, training, and documentation expertise
- Cross-functional problem solving
- Bilingual English/Spanish communication
- Willingness to start in a focused hourly or salaried role

## Core line

> I care more about returning to the right environment and doing useful work than I do about the starting title.

## Success conditions

Any one of these counts as campaign traction:

- Conversation with Elvis or Matt
- Referral to current HR
- Interview for an existing opening
- Identification of an upcoming role
- Defined short-term assignment
- Internal referral from Tugso
- Clear no, allowing clean closure
