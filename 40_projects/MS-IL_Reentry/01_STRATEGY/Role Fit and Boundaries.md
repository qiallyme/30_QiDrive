# Role Fit and Boundaries

## Strong entry roles

1. Interviewer / recruiting support
2. Payroll specialist
3. Account or client support
4. Office or branch support
5. Call center representative
6. Workforce operations support
7. HR operations support
8. Systems / technology support
9. Defined cross-functional floater

## Compensation structure

- Hourly: acceptable
- Salary: acceptable
- Overtime: acceptable
- Lower starting title: acceptable
- Growth later: likely, but not required as an opening promise

## Not acceptable right now

- Frontline supervisor
- Routine overnight calls
- 5:00 a.m. emergency coverage
- Undefined on-call responsibility
- “Help everywhere” with no primary role
- Manager accountability without authority or pay
- Several departments assigning work simultaneously

## Decision line

> Flexible on title and pay structure. Firm on sustainable scope and schedule.
