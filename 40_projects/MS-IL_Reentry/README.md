## Workspace rules

- Strategy lives in `01_STRATEGY`.
- Messages actually sent or ready to send live in `02_OUTREACH`.
- LinkedIn work lives in `03_LINKEDIN`.
- Website work lives in `04_WEB_PRESENCE`.
- Proof, resumes, and source notes live in `05_EVIDENCE`.
- All executable work is driven from `06_TASKS`.
- Contact and response history lives in `07_TRACKING`.
- Reusable structures live in `08_TEMPLATES`.
- Unsent rough wording lives in `09_DRAFTS`.
- Dead or superseded material goes to `99_ARCHIVE`.

## Naming rule

Use:
`YYYY-MM-DD - Person - Topic`

Example:
`2026-07-23 - Elvis - Reentry Email Follow-up.md`
