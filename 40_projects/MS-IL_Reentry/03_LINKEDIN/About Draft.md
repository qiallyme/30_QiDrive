# LinkedIn About

I make workforce operations work.

For 15+ years, I have worked across HR, staffing, operations, finance, and systems—usually where a branch, client account, process, or team needed to be stabilized. My strength is understanding how people, policy, data, and daily operations affect one another, then building a practical fix that people can actually use.

At MS-IL, I progressed from recruiter to senior account manager, HRIS trainer, and Operations HR Manager while supporting multi-account workforces of up to 500 employees. I partnered with branch and client leaders on recruiting, onboarding, employee relations, attendance, performance, workforce planning, compliance, safety, and day-to-day execution. I also led workers’ compensation and safety reforms that produced more than $1 million in cumulative savings and cost avoidance.

Since then, I have helped small businesses build HR foundations, implement HRIS workflows, strengthen employee records and document controls, correct compliance gaps, and automate work across HR, operations, finance, and leadership.

I am bilingual in English and Spanish and bring a rare combination of HR judgment, operational fluency, and technical systems capability.

I am currently open to focused W-2 roles in HR operations, HR business partnership, HRIS / people systems, staffing and recruiting operations, payroll, employee relations, account support, or workforce operations. I am comfortable entering as an individual contributor and growing with the organization. I care more about fit, stability, and meaningful work than title.
