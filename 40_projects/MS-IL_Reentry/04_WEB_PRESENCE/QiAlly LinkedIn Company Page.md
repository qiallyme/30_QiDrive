## Overview

QiAlly is a practical business operations studio led by Cody Rice-Velasquez. We help individuals, small businesses, and growing teams close the gaps between people, process, finance, and technology.

Our work includes HR operations, compliance foundations, HRIS and workflow design, tax and accounting support, document and knowledge systems, and practical automation. We turn scattered tools, inconsistent processes, and administrative bottlenecks into clear systems people can actually use.

QiAlly combines corporate-grade structure with direct, personal delivery.

Core areas:

- HR operations and compliance
- HRIS, records, and workforce workflows
- Tax and accounting support
- Process design and documentation
- Automation, AI, and business systems

Based in Indianapolis. Bilingual English / Spanish support available.
