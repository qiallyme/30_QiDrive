# Call Prep

## Thirty-second opening

> I am not trying to walk straight back into my old scope. I want a focused role, hourly or salaried, where I can contribute consistently and rebuild stability. I have worked across almost every part of the operation, so I can get useful quickly, but I am deliberately avoiding frontline supervision and constant on-call responsibility as my starting point. I care more about coming home, doing solid work, and growing naturally than I do about the initial title.

## Questions to ask

1. How is the company structured now?
2. Where are teams stretched?
3. Which roles are hardest to keep filled?
4. Are there current or planned openings in interviewing, payroll, account support, office support, call center, HR operations, or systems?
5. Who would the role report to?
6. What are the normal hours and on-call expectations?
7. What formal process should I complete next?

## Do not volunteer

- Full crisis timeline
- Internal political observations
- Old grievances
- Salary floor before hearing the range
- Claims that they “need” you

## Close

> Based on what you described, I am interested. What is the cleanest next step, and who owns it?
