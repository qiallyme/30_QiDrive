# Follow-up Scripts

## Three-business-day email

Reply all to the original thread.

Hi Elvis,

I wanted to bring this back to the top of your inbox and confirm that I am serious about exploring a return.

I am genuinely flexible regarding the division, title, and whether the role is hourly or salaried. My priority is finding a focused individual-contributor position where I can contribute consistently and build from there.

If there is a current or upcoming opening that may fit—or if someone else would be better positioned to discuss staffing needs with me—I would appreciate being pointed in the right direction.

I would also be happy to schedule a brief call whenever convenient.

Thank you,  
Cody

## Five-to-seven-business-day text to Elvis

Hey Elvis — I sent you an email last week about the possibility of returning to MS-IL and wanted to make sure it reached you. No need for a full response by text. I am serious about a focused individual-contributor role and flexible on hourly or salary. I would appreciate a short conversation when you have time.

## Check-in with Tugso

Have you heard anything about the note I sent Elvis and Matt? I do not want to over-chase it, but I do want to make sure I am approaching the right person and not missing an active need.

## If no openings

I understand. Please keep me in mind for interviewer, call center, payroll, account support, office support, systems support, or workforce operations openings. I am open to hourly or salaried work and can move quickly when something becomes available.

## If asked why lower level

I am optimizing for fit, stability, and longevity rather than title. I know what I can do, and I am confident broader responsibility will develop naturally if there is a need. Right now I want a focused role that I can perform consistently and build from.

## If offered supervisor role

I appreciate the confidence, but frontline supervision with overnight or unpredictable on-call coverage is not the right starting point for me. I would be happy to lead projects, train people, solve operational problems, or support a supervisor without holding that particular schedule and responsibility right now.

## Compensation

I am flexible and would rather start with the established range for the position. My priorities are consistency, fit, and room to grow. Once I understand the duties and schedule, I can give a more specific answer.
