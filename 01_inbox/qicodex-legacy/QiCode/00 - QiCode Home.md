## Authority Order

1. **QiCode Registry** identifies the official section number and canonical document.
2. **Active canonical documents** contain the enforceable body text.
3. **Working standards** govern organization until formally superseded.
4. **Archive and source files** preserve history but are not active doctrine.

## Package Boundaries

- Full QiLife doctrine has one canonical home per subject.
- The registry references doctrine; it does not duplicate it.
- Outline-only QiCode records remain outline-only rather than receiving invented content.
- Legacy material is quarantined until reviewed and promoted.
- The original compilation is preserved unchanged for audit and migration evidence.

## Known Reconciliation Item
