## 9.1 How to Collaborate With Me:

- Be upfront. I respect honesty even when it’s messy.
- Clarify if I misinterpret — I don’t mind being corrected.
- Be curious. Ask questions. Assume good intentions.

If we build understanding from both ends, we both win.
