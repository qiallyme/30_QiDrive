# QiEOS Protocol v2.0

## Chapter Purpose

QiEOS defines the unified structure of **QiOne**, integrating personal, business, and client systems into one modular, traceable framework.

QiEOS governs structure, QiCode defines law, and QiKBs embody practice. Each entity in QiOne holds a unique **QiDecimal ID** (`x.xx.xx` format), ensuring order, interoperability, and consistency across personal, business, and client knowledge realms.

## § 1.10.001 — Realm Structure

### Rule

QiOne is organized into numbered realms that separate intake, governance, personal knowledge, business knowledge, clients, apps, data, tools, docs, and archive storage.

### Meaning

The realm structure keeps the system modular, traceable, and portable. Each realm has a clear purpose so files and knowledge do not sprawl across random locations.

### Structure

```
QiOne/
├── 0_Inbox/                 → Temporary intake area
├── 1_QiEos/                 → Governance Realm (Constitution + Law)
│   ├── 1.10_Protocol/
│   │   └── QiEOS_Protocol_v2.0.md
│   ├── 1.20_QiCode/
│   ├── 1.30_Templates/
│   ├── 1.40_RAG/
│   ├── 1.50_Meta/
│   └── 1.90_ARCH/
│
├── 2_QsKb/                  → Personal Knowledge Base (QiSelf)
├── 3_QiKb/                  → Business Knowledge Base (QiAlly)
├── 4_Clients/               → Client Ecosystems
├── 5_Apps/                  → Application Layer
├── 6_Data/                  → Datasets and Indexes
├── 7_Tools/                 → Scripts and Utilities
├── 8_Docs/                  → Manuals and Publications
└── 9_ARCH/                  → Global Cold Storage
```

## § 1.10.002 — QiDecimal System

### Rule

Each folder or file carries a **QiDecimal ID** that denotes its realm, category, and atomic entry.

### Meaning

QiDecimal IDs create a shared namespace for folders, files, modules, pages, and knowledge entries.

### Format

- First digit: realm, `0–9`
- First decimal: subrealm or category
- Second decimal: atomic entry

### Example

`1.21.1` → QiEOS → QiCode → Title 1 → §1 “Principle of Awareness”

### Registry

The QiDecimal Registry is maintained in `/1_QiEos/1.50_Meta/QiCodex.csv`.

## § 1.10.004 — Versioning & Continuity

### Rule

Every update increments by minor version, deprecated files move to archive, and no deletion occurs without an archival entry.

### Minimum Requirements

- Every update increments by minor version: `v2.1`, `v2.2`, etc.
- Deprecated files move to `x.90_ARCH/`.
- No deletion occurs without an archival entry in `/1.50_Meta/INDEX.csv`.
- The QiCodex must reflect all assigned QiDecimal IDs.
- No ad-hoc numbering.

## § 1.10.006 — AI Governance

### Rule

All AI agents are indexed, scoped, and governed by their QiDecimal parent realm.

### Meaning

Agents inherit authority from the realm where they operate. Private materials are excluded unless explicitly authorized.

### Minimum Requirements

- AI agents are indexed in `/1.40_RAG/bots/*.yaml`.
- Agents inherit scope from their QiDecimal parent realm.
- Private materials remain excluded unless explicitly authorized.

## § 1.10.007 — Updates & Ratification

### Rule

Protocol changes move through draft, review, ratification, logging, and archival.

### Process

1. Propose draft under `/1.10_Protocol/Drafts/`.
2. Review via Qi-Master or manual audit.
3. Ratify and rename to `QiEOS_Protocol_vX.Y.md`.
4. Log update in `/1.50_Meta/QiCodex.csv`.
5. Archive prior versions under `/1.10_Protocol/Archive/`.
